Privacy First

Your files are processed entirely on your local machine and are never uploaded to a server. Only the minimum data necessary is sent to external services for optional features:

  • Search: Text queries.
  • Elevation Profiles: Path coordinates (when not in file).
  • Routing: Start, end, and via point coordinates.
  • Strava: Authorization and activity requests.
